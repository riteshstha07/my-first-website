Gamcha Jatra Mahautsav 2082



  
     






  

    
   
    
   
    
  
    
   
    

  



    
Jatra Mahautsab 2082 was organized by Gamcha Youth Club. It was successfully finished. GYC has entertain all the viewers with the help of different actors & local people dances. GYC has invited many National Artist,Different bands, Dancers on Gamcha Jatra Mahautsab.

Installing Light in Pillers 
    for Local People.




  

    
   
    
   
    
  
    
   
    












 




  



  

   According to the locals of Gamcha, They are afraid to walk at evening because of the darkness in the main road. So, due to this reason Gamcha Youth Club have installed lights on the pillars. Locals are really happy now.
